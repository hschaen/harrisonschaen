Harrison Schaen (“20/20”) is an American-born record producer and recording artist. Since 2008, 20/20 has recorded, produced and performed at some of the world’s biggest stages alongside some of the world’s best talent including Cali Swag District (319/SME), The New Boys (Warner Bros./Asylum), GQ Ryda (Def Jam/N’Credible), HERO (Def Jam/N’Credible) and Lana (Atlantic).
